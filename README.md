# 📊 BI Dashboard Analysis of Customer Sales

### ✅ Problem statement:

-	Domain – E-Commerce & Sales

In recent times, due to strategic moves by other competitors and ineffective decision-making by management, our client is losing market sales and revenue in the e-commerce business. 
As a strategic move, the management wants to develop a comprehensive BI dashboard to ‘Analyse’ and ‘Track’ customer sales for the Year 2025. It will help them understand and analyse their sales monthly as well as quarterly

### ✅ Data sources:

Data Sources are already available in Excel CSV files, extracted from the client’s main database server. The CSV files contain up-to-date information.

### ✅ Project Execution Steps:

1. Understanding the business acumen:
   
- Understanding business requirements and framing the right questions that will lead to better insight generation

2. Discussion of Problem Statement & Solution design:
   
- Mock-up review discussion with stakeholders, so the project aligns with their views and needs.
- Designing a dummy mock-up and further discussion with stakeholders.

#### 🔎Key Metrics required by Stakeholder:

-	Total Orders 
-	Average Order Value (AOV)
-	Top Customers & Payment Modes
-	Top Performing Regions

3.	Imported those CSV files to Power BI for further Transformation and analysis using Query Editor:
   
-	Import CSV files to Power BI & transform in Query Editor
-	Perform calculations, remove errors & null values.
-	Establish required relationship using Data Modelling

4.	Performed DAX Measures:
    
-	Created calculated columns - AOV, Date with Days, Month, Qtr.
-	Created numerous DAX measures

5.	Build a dashboard:
- Created a BI Dashboard from scratch using Bar charts, Donut charts, KPI indicators, and many more.
- And visualized the key performance indicators.

### ✅ Some quick Insights from the Dashboard:

-	Total Revenue of 4.39 Lacs earned for the Year 2025
-	Sum of AOV is approx. 1.20 lacs 
-	Top customer is Harivansh, contributing 10k in sales & Priyanka emerged as the Top Customer to maximum orders
-	Top State: Madhya Pradesh contributes 19.87 % of ‘Total Sales’, with ‘Indore’ being the top city.
-	The month of March is the ‘Highest Order Month.’
-	Months of March & November performed well in terms of Revenue, with July being the lowest.
